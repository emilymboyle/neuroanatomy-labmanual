10 The Sensory Systems

In this laboratory session, we will study aspects of the anatomy of the visual, olfactory, gustatory, and auditory systems. Below, you will be presented with a number of figures and asked to label or color certain structures in each figure.

10.1.1 Eyball Dissection

10.1.2 The Eye

In Figure 10.1 , label the following structures:

  1. Color the lens of the eye in yellow.
  2. Color the vitreous body in pink.
  3. Color the iris in brown.
  4. Color the retina in green.
  5. Color the sclera in blue.
  6. Color the ciliary body in red.
  7. Label the structures pointed to by the black lines with the appropriate anatomical name.

Figure 10.1: A horizontal section of the eyball.
