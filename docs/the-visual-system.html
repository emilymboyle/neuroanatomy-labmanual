2 The Visual System

In this laboratory session, we will study the anatomy of the visual system. Below, you will be presented with a number of figures and asked to label or color certain structures in each figure.

2.1 The Eye

  1. Color the lens of the eye in yellow.
  2. Color the vitreous body in pink.
  3. Color the iris in brown.
  4. Color the retina in green.
  5. Color the sclera in blue.
  6. Color the ciliary body in red.
  7. Label the structures pointed to by the black lines with the appropriate anatomical name.

Figure 2.1: A horizontal section of the eyball.
